Key regulations the app must handle:

- **PPN (VAT)**: 11% (UU HPP), with 12% transition for luxury goods (PP 44/2024, PMK 131/2024). e-Faktur mandatory for PKP.
- **PPh 21 (Employee tax)**: TER method since Jan 2024 (PP 58/2023, PMK 168/2023). December annual true-up. PTKP categories TK/0 through K/3.
- **PPh 23**: 2% on services (PMK 141/2015). Bukti potong required.
- **PPh 4(2)**: Final tax — construction 1.75-6%, rent 10%, MSME 0.5% (PP 23/2018, time-limited).
- **Coretax**: New DJP system launched Jan 2025 (PMK 81/2024). Replaces e-Filing, e-Billing, e-Faktur.
- **BPJS Kesehatan**: 5% of salary (4% employer + 1% employee), capped at Rp 12M salary.
- **BPJS Ketenagakerjaan**: JKK 0.24-1.74%, JKM 0.3%, JHT 3.7%+2%, JP 2%+1% (capped ~Rp 10.4M).
- **THR**: 1 month salary for 12+ months service, prorated for 1-12 months. Pay 7 days before religious holiday.
- **Severance**: PP 35/2021 — up to 9 months pesangon, multiplier depends on termination reason.
- **UMK/UMP**: Regional minimum wages, formula per PP 51/2023.
- **UU PDP**: Data privacy law (UU 27/2022) — consent, 3x24h breach notification, 2% revenue fine.
- **SPT Deadlines**: PPh monthly by 20th, PPN by end of month, annual badan by Apr 30.
