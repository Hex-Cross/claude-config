name: PatuhIn Business Model
description: Revenue streams, pricing tiers, growth targets, and competitive moat for PatuhIn compliance platform
type: project
---

**Revenue streams:** SaaS subscription (70%), per-filing fees (15%), AI doc generation (10%), consultant marketplace (5% - Phase 2).

**Pricing:**
- Gratis: Rp 0 (1 product, lead gen)
- Starter: Rp 299K/month (10 products, 3 users)
- Professional: Rp 999K/month (50 products, AI assistant)
- Enterprise: Rp 5-15 Juta/month (unlimited, API, SLA)

**Target:** 100 paying customers by month 6, 500 by month 12, 2000 by month 24.

**Key deadline:** October 17, 2026 — mandatory halal certification for all products in Indonesia. This is the primary marketing urgency driver.

**Why:** LTV:CAC ratio of 9.6:1, payback period 3.3 months. Compliance is mandatory = sticky customers, low churn.

**How to apply:** Features should prioritize the halal certification workflow first (Phase 1 wedge), then BPOM, then TKDN/PDP. The Oct 2026 deadline is the growth engine.
